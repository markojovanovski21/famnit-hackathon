
Car Suspension / Speed Bump Analyzer - Google Ecosystem Integration Ideas
=========================================================================

CORE IDEA
---------
A web platform where users upload a side-view video of a car driving over a speed bump.
The system:
- tracks wheel movement
- measures vibration/suspension oscillation
- analyzes resonance
- plots graphs
- calculates optimal crossing speed
- generates reports and insights

10. SIGNAL PROCESSING / PHYSICS ENGINE
--------------------------------------

Core analysis:
- FFT frequency analysis
- damping ratio estimation
- oscillation amplitude
- resonance detection
- wheel displacement tracking

Important equations:

forcing frequency:
f = v / lambda

natural frequency:
f_n = (1 / (2*pi)) * sqrt(k / m)

Possible outputs:
- resonance speed
- safest speed
- smoothest speed
- damping quality
- comfort score
