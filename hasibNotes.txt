We made a program that detects wheels. 
Next to wheels we also detect the wheel well, by projecting a vertical line from the mid-point of the upper line of the bounding box a detected wheel.
By doing this we ensure to get accurate data regarding the wheel vibration/oscillation.
We save this information regarding the wheel-wheelwell in a csv file for later graph plotting. 
EQE 01 has 12*60= 720 frames.
Wheels appear at 323 and end at 510.
